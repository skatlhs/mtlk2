Why Tinting Might Be The Right Choice For You

Visual Statement

It’s bold. It’s classy. It’s cool! We stock a wide variety of film shades to compliment any vehicle’s exterior.

Natural Disaster Protection

In the event of a natural disaster, the adhesive layer on window films can help hold shattered glass together, protecting you and other occupants from flying glass!

Satisfaction Guaranteed

Lifetime warranty means no peeling, bubbling, cracking, adhesive failure, delamination or installation problems. We even cover excessive fading and change of colour. Replacement product and labour is included, so you pay nothing as long as you own the vehicle.

Protection From The Sun

The sun’s ultraviolet(UV) rays are known to cause skin cancer, the most common form of cancer in North America. Our efficient window films are engineered to block 99.9% of the sun’s harmful UV rays!

Reduces Glare

Annoying glare from the sun or headlights at night can blind your vision while driving. Because of our film’s anti-glare technology, visibility is improved and eyestrain is reduced and your drive is much safer by tinting your vehicle’s side and rear windows.

Increases Security

The ability of window films to hold glass together creates a bond that makes it extremely difficult for a thief to gain access to your vehicle.

Live Cooler

Immediately after installation, you will feel a difference on how much cooler your home feels. We carry window films that are designed to reject up to 79% of the sun’s total solar energy!

Energy Savings

By reducing the temperature in your residential or commercial property, the use of an AC unit or heat source becomes unnecessary.. The result? A great decrease on your monthly hydro or gas bill.

Accident Protection

In any incident of an automotive accident, the adhesive layer on window films can help hold shattered glass together, protecting you and passengers from flying glass!
